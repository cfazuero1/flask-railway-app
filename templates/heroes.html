Meet the Heroes

The champions of the Hacker Escape Room. Each with a unique mission, each fighting their own cyber battles.

Nguyen Lan aka The GRC Guardian

From: Vietnam

Role: Governance, Risk & Compliance Specialist

Company: Aquila Global Holdings (Melbourne)

At Aquila Global Holdings, a sprawling multinational, Nguyen Lan uncovers a quiet insider ring abusing weak policies and loophole-ridden controls to siphon sensitive information. She maps policy-to-control gaps, strengthens governance, and builds insider-threat playbooks to expose the manipulators and restore accountability across the enterprise.

Focus: Policy design, control testing, insider-threat investigations, audit readiness.

Chinedu Okoro aka The Pentest Phantom

From: Nigeria

Role: Red Team / Penetration Tester

Engagement: Kestrel Biotech full-scope assessment

Brought in by Kestrel Biotech, Chinedu blends social engineering with sharp exploit chains: phishing pretexts, badge tailgating, and lateral movement from a forgotten lab subnet into production. His job is to break in before attackers do and leave a trail of fixes that close every door he opened.

Focus: Social engineering, exploitation, privilege escalation, reporting.

Alejandro Salazar aka The Reluctant Cyber Analyst

From: Colombia

Role: Cybersecurity Analyst

After escaping a ruthless hacker gang in Colombia, Alejandro is kidnapped and trapped in the basement of Q1 Tower, Australia. A bomb ticks at the top. He has 12 hours to climb floor by floor, solving CTF-style challenges that unlock the elevator until he reaches the device and disables it. Brilliant, reluctant, and under pressure, Alejandro turns puzzle solving into survival.

Focus: DFIR, log analysis, puzzle solving, incident response under pressure.
