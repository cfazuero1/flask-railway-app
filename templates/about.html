About Us

Who we are and why this exists.

Hacker Escape Room

Hi! I am Christian Azuero. I built this project to combine three things I love: Capture the Flag (CTF), Escape Rooms, and blasting Music while working. For me, the first two are thrilling. They push you to think under pressure, collaborate, enjoy, and solve cleverly designed puzzles. The third one is optional depeding of your taste.

This site is a space for the cybersecurity community to challenge themselves through story-driven CTFs. The main focus areas are GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance), Cyber Analysis, and Penetration Testing.

Whether you are practicing fundamentals or exploring advanced scenarios, I hope these rooms are both fun and educational. See you at the top floor.
